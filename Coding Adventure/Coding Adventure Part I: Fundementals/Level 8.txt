3.times ->
    turn left
    step 15

# Explanation: Turns left and walks 15 steps. Since this is done repetitively 3 times, we could simply use a loop.
# Notes: When we use a times -> loop, everything contained within the loop (shown by four spaces of indentification) will be repeated by the specified number of times.
